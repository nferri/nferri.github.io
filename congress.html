Top 5 Wealthiest Congressional Leaders

Data acquired from Roll Call

Darrell Issa

 With a whopping $283.3 million net worth, Representative Darrell Issa of California is the richest member of Congress. He is a Replubican who made his riches in the 1990s while leading Directed Electronics Inc., a Vista-based manufacturer of vehicle antitheft devices that he created. Learn more about Darrel Issa.

Greg Gianforte

 A surprising second, Representative Greg Gianforte of Montana current net worth is estimated at $135.7 million. He is also a Replubican and has profitted in the technology and data analytics industries. Learn more about Greg Gianforte.

Jared Polis

 A close second to Gianforte, Representative Jared Polis of Colorado has a net worth of $122.6 million. Jared Polis is the richest Democrat in Congress who founded American Information Systems, co-founded ProFlowers.com and BlueMontain.com. Learn more about Jared Polis.

David Trott

Representative David Trott of Michigan has a net worth of $119.1 million. Trott is a Republican and a real son-of-a-bitch. He transformed his parent’s small law office, Trott & Trott, into a real estate behemoth with thousands of portfolios of bad mortgages. At the height of the last decade’s housing crisis, the company evicted almost 80,000 a year in Michigan alone. WHO ELECTED THIS DUDE?! Learn more about David Trott.

Michael McCaul

 Closing the top 5 list is Representative Michael McCaul of Texas who has a net worth of $113 million. Michael McCaul founded Clear Channel Communications, which transformed into iHeartCommunications. Learn more about Michael McCaul.
